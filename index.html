Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about partnering with K-FoodLAB

You only need approximately 1㎡ (10 square feet) of space. This can be a corner in your existing store, a counter area, or any underutilized retail section. No kitchen construction or plumbing required — just a standard electrical outlet.

No specialized staff or chefs required! The LAZO AI cooking machine handles all cooking automatically. Operation is as simple as: (1) open food pouch, (2) place in machine, (3) press button. Cooking takes 3 minutes 30 seconds, and the machine self-cleans. Anyone can operate it with minimal 15-minute training.

The minimum MOQ section package is $1,812 AUD (excluding equipment), or $3,612 AUD total including 2 LAZO machines. However, we offer a 12-month installment plan for equipment, reducing your initial cash outlay to approximately $1,950 AUD (consumables + first installment). This low barrier allows you to test the business model with minimal financial risk.

From contract signing to first sale: typically 2-3 weeks. Timeline includes: (1) Equipment delivery and installation (1 week), (2) Product stocking and setup (3-5 days), (3) Basic training (1 day). Our "plug and play" model means you can start generating revenue almost immediately after setup.

We offer a 1:1 Exchange A/S service during your installment period. If equipment malfunctions, we immediately swap it with a replacement unit (Exchange & Repair model). This minimizes downtime and ensures business continuity. After the warranty period, we maintain spare inventory for rapid repairs and modular component replacement.

Absolutely! Our section-based model is designed for scalability. After validating profitability at your first location (typically 2-3 months), you can add sections to other stores or become a distributor for your region. We support multi-location partnerships and offer volume discounts for larger orders.

Our goal is to ensure healthy market density without oversaturation — protecting your investment while expanding K-FoodLAB's presence strategically.
